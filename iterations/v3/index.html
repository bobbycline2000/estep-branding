Built for people.
Built to last.

Thirty-five years. Forty-eight restaurants. One conviction that has never wavered.

When Estep & Company opened its first restaurant in Columbus, Indiana in 1990, the math was simple. Take care of the people who run the store, and the store takes care of itself. Three and a half decades later, the math has not changed — and neither has the conviction behind it.

Today we operate forty-eight restaurants across Indiana, Kentucky, and Florida — under four nationally known brands and one operating standard. Twelve hundred teammates. Twenty-two-year tenures behind the counter. A bench of leaders who started as crew. The proof is on the floor.
