— Recommendation All three keep the navy / gold / cream palette steady. Iteration 03 — Editorial Press is the strongest pick for a heritage-operator brand like Estep & Company — it reads like an institution, the food still pops, and the ledger format scales to 100+ stores without breaking. Iteration 02 is the close second if you want a more modern, operator-bold feel. Iteration 01 is the safest evolution of what's live today.
